Wish Week is an annual week-long event celebrated by schools around the district, the purpose of this event is to grant the wish of a special kid in Colorado. This event is associated with deals, auctions and other sponsored events. For this year our kid is Lucy, to learn more about Lucy look down below!

About Lucy

Lucy is 7 years old and her favorite colors are purple & pink.
She enjoys the board game Skull King and Candyland she also really enjoys playing Roblox and Minecraft.
She enjoys basketball, jumping on her trampoline, and camping with friends and family.
